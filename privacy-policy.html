Privacy Policy for trainer

Effective Date: 2025-08-08
App Developer: ready to train

Overview

trainer. ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile fitness application collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

Key Point: Your personal workout data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or share your personal information with anyone.

Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

  • Workout History: Records of your completed workouts including completion times, percentages, and workout themes
  • Usage Statistics: Your workout streaks, preferences, and exercise statistics
  • App Settings: Your personal app configuration and preferences

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect personal identifying information (name, email, phone number)
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, or other personal files
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use analytics or advertising trackers
  • We do not create user accounts or profiles

How We Use Information

Local Data Usage

  • Display your workout history and statistics
  • Track your fitness progress and streaks
  • Personalize your workout experience
  • Maintain app preferences between sessions

Data Retention

  • Workout data is stored locally on your device only
  • We automatically limit storage to your most recent 100 workouts
  • You can clear this data by uninstalling the app

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share, sell, or transmit your personal data to any third parties.

Workout Content Delivery

  • We download workout content from our content delivery network (Amazon CloudFront)
  • This connection only downloads workout instructions - no personal data is sent
  • No user information is transmitted during content downloads

Device Permissions

Our app requests the following permissions:

  • Internet Access: To download daily workout content
  • Notifications: To provide a interactable banner during workouts
  • Background Audio: To provide voice guidance during workouts
  • Wake Lock: To prevent your device from sleeping during active workouts

Data Security

  • All personal data remains on your device
  • We use industry-standard practices for secure content delivery
  • No user accounts or passwords are required
  • Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features

Your Rights and Choices

  • Data Access: View your workout history anytime within the app
  • Data Deletion: Uninstall the app to remove all stored data
  • Notifications: Disable notifications in your device settings
  • Content Updates: The app requires internet access for workout content - this cannot be disabled

Children's Privacy

trainer. does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is intended for general fitness use by individuals capable of performing exercise routines safely.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify users of material changes through app updates. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of any changes.
